How does it work?

Cat enters the toilet and PIR sensor is activated. After 2 minutes DHT11 sensor sends the data to RaspberryPi. This data is being uploaded and stored in MySQL database. Everytime you refresh this webpage, last 20 entries are visualised above. Please note that the humidity and temperature values refer to humidity and temperature of the air inside of cats toilet, not the actual "product"
